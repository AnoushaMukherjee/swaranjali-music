About Swaranjali School of Music

In 1999, with the blessings of her guru, Pandit Jasraj, Vasu founded the Swaranjali School of Music in Bellevue, Washington. The School currently trains a large number of students, ranging from ages six to sixty-five.

In 2000, Vasu also founded the Northwest’s first South Asian children’s choir, which is still active and performs four to five times every year. It is a source of great pride and satisfaction to Vasu that her students regularly perform Hindustani music at their schools and at community events throughout the Greater Seattle area.
